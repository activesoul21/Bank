Our Story

NexBank was founded in 2004 with one simple belief that every Ghanaian deserves access to world-class banking regardless of their background or income level.

From a single branch in Accra, we have grown into a trusted institution serving over 500,000 customers across Ghana with personal banking, business solutions, and investment products.
